Our Commitment to Transparency

Ononto does NOT pay any salary or compensation to its members or volunteers. Every member contributes ₹50 per month from their own pocket. This monthly contribution is used exclusively to cover operational expenses such as travel, packaging, and logistics — so that 100% of public donations go directly to feeding communities without any deduction for overheads. Join us knowing your time and ₹50/month create real, direct impact.

Understand First

How Membership Works

We believe in full transparency. Here's exactly what it means to be an Ononto member.

01

Volunteer Your Time

Members voluntarily dedicate their time to Ononto's programmes — food distribution, events, outreach, and more. There is no salary or payment.

02

₹50 Monthly Contribution

Each member contributes ₹50 per month from their own pocket. This covers travel costs, packaging, and logistics for our programmes — not personal expenses.

03

Donations Stay Pure

Because members self-fund operations via ₹50/month, every rupee donated by the public goes 100% to the cause — meals, food supplies, and community impact. Zero overhead deduction.

04

Be Part of the Family

You get a real role, real responsibility, and real community. Work alongside passionate people who share your values and create lasting friendships while making a difference.

Please Read Before Applying

  • No salary, stipend, or financial compensation is provided to any member or volunteer at Ononto.
  • Members contribute ₹50 per month to cover programme operational costs (travel, packaging, logistics).
  • Public donations are never used for member expenses. The ₹50/month ensures donation money goes 100% to communities.
  • Membership is open to anyone aged 16+ who is passionate about community service.
  • You may leave the organisation at any time with no obligations.
  • By submitting this form, you confirm you understand and accept these terms.
