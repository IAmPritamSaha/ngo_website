Our Story

About Us

Founded in 2021, Ononto has been at the heart of community transformation in Howrah, West Bengal.

01

Our Mission

Committed to fostering hope and uplifting communities through targeted food assistance. We alleviate hunger by providing nutritious meals, promoting dignity and equality for all.

02

Our Belief

We believe in the transformative power of compassion and community. Small acts of kindness can drive significant change. Our volunteers ensure no one is left behind.

03

Our Vision

We aim to expand services beyond food to education and healthcare, fostering a future where every individual thrives and communities flourish together.
